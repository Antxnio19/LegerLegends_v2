Help & Documentation

Welcome to the help page. Below are some frequently asked questions and answers to help you navigate the system.

FAQs

  • How do I create a new user account?
    Go to "User Management" -> "Create User". Fill in the required details and click "Submit".
  • How do I approve a pending account?
    Navigate to "Account Approval" under "User Management". Select the account to approve or reject.
  • How do I view reports?
    Under the "Reports" menu, you can view different types of reports, including user activity, password expiration, and login attempts.
  • How do I submit an IT ticket?
    You can submit an IT ticket by clicking "IT Ticket" in the navigation bar.

Contact Support

If you have any further questions, feel free to contact the IT department at support@ledgerlegend.com.
